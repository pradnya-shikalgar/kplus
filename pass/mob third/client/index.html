Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen or battery replacements take about 30-60 minutes. More complex repairs may take longer, but we'll always give you an accurate time estimate before starting.

Do you offer any warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 12-month warranty covering both parts and labor. If you experience any issues with your repair within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

What areas do you service?

We currently service all areas in Islampur and surrounding regions within 20km. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, please call or WhatsApp us to confirm.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), and major credit/debit cards. Payment is due upon completion of the repair.

Do I need to backup my data before repair?

While most repairs don't affect your data, we always recommend backing up important data as a precaution. For water damage repairs, we offer data recovery services if needed.
